Spirited Away is a 2001 anime film written and directed by Hayao Miyazaki. It follows the story of a girl, named Chihiro. Chihiro and her parents are moving to a new home and while on the road, they decide to make a detour and thus our story begins. They make a stop by a seemingly empty town, but when night comes, the town floods with food and people. Little did Chihiro know, that whoever eats the food there becomes a pig and transports to the spirit world. Thankfully Chihiro doesn't eat any of tthe food, only her parents, and from then on Chihiro tries to hide the fact that she is a living human, and tries to save her parents. She makes new friends on the road, which help her and eventually both she and her parents are saved and return to their original world.
