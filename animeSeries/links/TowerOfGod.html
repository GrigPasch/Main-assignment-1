Tower of God follows the sotry of Bam the 16th, a human boy, that wants to enter the Tower of God, a place that is said to grant people who try and climb tit every wish. One day, his "friend: Rachel, enters the tower and he follows her inside. Inside he will soon find out, that in order to climb the tower you need to do many things and be powerfull. The whole series is based on his journey to becoming stronger and climbing the tower while searching for his "friend" for the first few chapters.
